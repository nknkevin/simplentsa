# Branding Assets

Place your custom branding files in this directory:

- **logo.png** - Your company logo (recommended size: 200x60px or similar aspect ratio)
- **favicon.ico** - Your website favicon (16x16 or 32x32 pixels)
- **login-bg.jpg** - Background image for the login page (recommended: 1920x1080px)

## Example from jendientsa.co.ke style

For a professional look similar to jendientsa.co.ke:
- Use a clean, modern logo with your brand colors
- Choose a high-quality stock photo for the login background (vehicles, technology, or abstract patterns work well)
- Update the primary color in `config/config.php` to match your brand
